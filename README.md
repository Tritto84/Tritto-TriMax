This repo is to track the progress of adding the madmax tool changer to a Voron Trident. I am using Klipper-Backup to directly make changes as I often forget to keep records of what I have changed.

Currently the trident build follows the script with the following inclusion/mods:
- 9mm belts
- [eddy coil probe](https://github.com/bigtreetech/Eddy)
- [nudge probe](https://github.com/zruncho3d/nudge)
- canbus setup as per Esoterical [voron_canbus](https://github.com/Esoterical/voron_canbus/blob/main/README.md)
- Electronics; Ocotpus v1.1, BTTPi V1.2, 4 x EBB42 v1.2 and the usual SSR and PSU
- The toolheads are [AntHead Standard](https://github.com/PrintersForAnts/AntHead/blob/main/README.md)
-  with 2 Orbital extruders and 2 Anthead [WW-BMG](https://github.com/PrintersForAnts/AntHead/blob/main/UserMods/Wristwatch-BMG/README.md) as I had components leftover from building stealthburners

The build is using materials and hardware I had left laying arround from a couple of donor T-Rex Printers. I had them both running on canbus with octopus v1.1 boards with EBB42 v1.2 toolhead boards. I will be following the build VODs of [Steve](https://github.com/stvptrsn) from his youtube Tridex to TriMax Playlist. 

Once I have the printer working as intended I will scale the build back to a print volume of 300mm³ and complete the enclosure and serial. 

Not great with using github, but If I made modification to other files I will try and upload them here first and then work out where they should go.
